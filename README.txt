This is a finals project on Sterilisation in Denmark 1929-67 via data tabels found in Lene Koch's Tvangssterilisation i Danmark 1929-67,2000, and Rigsdagstidende 1919-20, Tillæg A, Col.3663-70 for data from the people counts of 1916 with focus on Invalids of women and men, and particular with focus on the group "Sindssyge og Aandssvage" (the insane and feeble-minded). The population counts that were used here was found Folketællinger 1916 which was used as comparison.

Comprehensive R analysis and visualization toolkit. The software processes the historical data tables. Generates multi-panel ggplot2 visualizations stratified by sex, age groups, institutional residency, diagnostic classifications (mental illness & intellectual disability, neuroses, epilepsy, physical illness, etc.), eugenic indication status, patient characteristics (late maturation discharge planning, satisfaction), and geographic distribution across island dioceses and Jutland institutions. Also, bar and graphs (gender) plots for the invalids of the 1916 counts.


Tabel's used and made:
1. Invaliditetsgrupper1919 i forbindelse med invaliderente lov.csv
2. Insane_and_feeble_sex.csv
3. tabel_invalide_in_1916_in_relation_to_the_population_count_1916_csv.csv
4. compare_procent_of_invalids_men_and_women_with_total_population_per_area.csv
5. Tabel_1_to_17_three_laws_involving_sterilisation.csv
